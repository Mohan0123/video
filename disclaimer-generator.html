How to Use the Disclaimer Generator

Creating a disclaimer page is crucial for limiting liability. We offer two output formats: HTML (for your website source code) and Plain Text (for documents).

  1. Enter Details: Fill in your Company Name, Website Name, URL, and Contact Email.
  2. Generate: Click "Generate Disclaimer".
  3. Choose Format:
    • HTML Code: Select this tab if you want code with tags (<p>, <h2>) to paste into WordPress (Text view) or HTML files.
    • Plain Text: Select this tab if you want clean text to paste into Word, Docs, or a visual editor.
  4. Copy: Click "Copy to Clipboard" and paste it where needed.

Why Your Website Needs a Disclaimer

A disclaimer is a legal statement that limits your liability for the outcome of the use of your site. Using a disclaimer generator ensures you cover essential legal bases.

  • Legal Protection: It protects you from lawsuits regarding the accuracy of your content.
  • Third-Party Liability: It clarifies that you are not responsible for the content of external links.
  • AdSense Approval: Google requires clear policies regarding content validity and user responsibility.
