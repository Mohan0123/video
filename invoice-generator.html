How to Use the VIP Tool Invoice Generator

  1. Enter Business Details: Fill in your company details in the "From" section and your client's details in the "Bill To" section.
  2. Add Invoice Items: List the services or products provided. Enter the description, quantity, and rate for each. The tool calculates the amount automatically.
  3. Set Tax: If applicable, enter the tax percentage (GST, VAT, etc.) to calculate the final total.
  4. Download or Print: Click the "Print / Save as PDF" button. To save as a file, select "Save as PDF" in the print destination options.
